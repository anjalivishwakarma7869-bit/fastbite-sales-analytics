# 🍔 Fast Food Restaurant Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales performance of a Fast Food Restaurant using Microsoft Excel. The objective is to understand customer purchasing behavior, identify top-performing food items, evaluate regional profitability, and track monthly sales trends.

The dashboard was built using Excel Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPIs, and interactive visualizations to convert raw transactional data into meaningful business insights.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Fast food restaurants generate hundreds of transactions daily. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to:

- Identify top-selling products
- Track profit performance
- Understand customer purchasing patterns
- Evaluate regional performance
- Monitor the impact of discounts on profitability

This project aims to solve these challenges through data analysis and dashboard reporting.

# 💡 Key Insights

## Sales Insights

- Chicken Wings generated the highest revenue.
- Pizza and Pasta are also strong performers.
- Cold Drinks generated the lowest sales.

## Customer Insights

- A small group of customers contributes a significant portion of revenue.
- Sneha Jain is the highest-spending customer.

## Regional Insights

- East Region generated the highest profit.
- South Region generated the lowest profit.

## Discount Insights

- Average discount offered is approximately 5%.
- Higher discounts reduce profit margins.

---

# 📋 Recommendations

## Product Strategy

- Promote Chicken Wings, Pizza, and Pasta through combo offers.
- Review pricing strategy for low-performing products.

## Customer Retention

- Launch loyalty programs for top customers.
- Offer personalized discounts to repeat buyers.

## Regional Growth

- Analyze why the East region performs best.
- Apply similar sales strategies in other regions.

## Profit Optimization

- Limit excessive discounting.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-margin products.
